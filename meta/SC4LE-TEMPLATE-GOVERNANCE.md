# SC4LE Template Governance Model  
### Governance of Templates, Schemas, Structures & Reusable Content Patterns  
© 2026 SC4LE Limited

---

## 1. Purpose of This Document

This document defines how **SC4LE templates** are created, governed, versioned, and maintained across:

- the SC4LE Standards Repository  
- the SC4LE website  
- programme pages  
- service pages  
- diagrams  
- documents  
- AI‑generated content  

Templates ensure SC4LE content is:

- consistent  
- governed  
- reusable  
- principle‑aligned  
- brand‑aligned  
- structurally coherent  

This file governs **templates**, not the SC4LE Standard itself.

---

## 2. Relationship to the SC4LE Standard

Templates must always reflect and reinforce:

- the SC4LE Operating Model  
- SC4LE Shared Principles  
- SC4LE Brand Governance  
- SC4LE Diagram Governance  
- SC4LE Messaging Governance  
- SC4LE Website Content Matrix  
- SC4LE Content Governance  

Templates are **derived from the standard**, not creative reinterpretations.

---

## 3. Template Governance Principles

### 3.1 Consistency Over Creativity  
Templates exist to ensure consistency, not stylistic variation.
